About Me!

Aloha! I’m Donn, a born-and-raised Hilo local with over five decades of deep-rooted connection to the Island of Hawaii. As a seasoned real estate agent, my lifelong passion for this vibrant community drives me to help clients navigate the unique opportunities of Hawaii’s dynamic property market.

Growing up in Hilo, I’ve witnessed the island’s evolution firsthand—from its lush rainforests and volcanic landscapes to its thriving neighborhoods and hidden gems. My 50 years of living here have gifted me with an intimate understanding of the island’s diverse regions, whether you’re drawn to the serene charm of Hilo, the sunny shores of Kona, the rolling pastures of Waimea, or the tranquil beauty of Puna.

Specializing in the Big Island, I blend local wisdom with professional expertise to provide tailored guidance for buyers and sellers alike. Whether you’re searching for a dream home, an investment property, or aiming to sell with confidence, I prioritize your goals with the utmost care and respect for Hawaii’s culture, land, and heritage.

Beyond real estate, my commitment to this island runs deep. My family’s roots here span generations, and I’m dedicated to preserving the spirit of aloha in every transaction. When you work with me, you’re not just getting an agent—you’re gaining a trusted advisor who values relationships, transparency, and your vision for the future.

Let’s explore the possibilities together. Contact me today, and let’s turn your Big Island real estate aspirations into reality.
