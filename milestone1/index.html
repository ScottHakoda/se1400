Website Summary

What content will the site contain?

My father(Donn) is in real estate and I plan to revamp his existing website.

Where will you get all of the content for your site (ie. images & text)?

I will keep the contents of the website primarily similar to what he currently has with the additional touchups here and there. Resources I will use will be from google, the existing website, or from Donn himself.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

Anyone looking to buy or sell a house on Hawaii Island would care about the site.

Why should a site be built for this?

A site should be built for this because it provides for a way to get into contact with a realtor for those inquiring about home ownership in Hawaii.

What would you like users to do at your site?

I would like users to be able to get a grasp of who Donn is and be able to contact him if they wish to further inquire on buying or selling a house on Hawaii Island. I also would like if users were able to view current listing Donn has available through the website.

What is the goal of the site?

I want users to gather an idea of who Donn is and be able to decide if they want to take their real estate ventures to him.
